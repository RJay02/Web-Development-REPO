Breaking Bad Overview

This series follows the exploits of Walter White, a modest high school chemistry teacher, who discovers a new purpose in life when he learns he has terminal cancer and turns to a life of crime to provide for his family.

Key Information

  • Story revolves around Walter White, a modest high school chemistry teacher who transforms into a ruthless kingpin in the local drug trade.
  • Set in Albuquerque, New Mexico.
  • Timeline between 2008 and 2010.
